## Overview

Conducted a comprehensive audit and strategic analysis of Old Greg's Tavern Discord community, examining member engagement, retention patterns, and growth opportunities across multiple dimensions.

## Project Scope

### Data Analysis
Analyzed community health through 15+ key metrics:
- **Guild joins by source and invite link tracking**
- **Message activity and voice participation patterns**
- **Member retention and activation rates**
- **Platform engagement metrics**
- **Discord tenure analysis**
- **Communicator behavior patterns**
- **Guild leavers and churn analysis**

### Deliverables
- **Server Audit Presentation**: Professional deck with data visualizations (iterated through 8 versions based on stakeholder feedback)
- **Strategic Recommendations**: Actionable insights for improving community engagement
- **FAQ Documentation**: Community management resources
- **ELI5 Approach**: Made complex analytics accessible to non-technical stakeholders

## Tools & Technologies

- **Data Collection**: Discord analytics exports (15 CSV files)
- **Analysis**: CSV data processing and pattern identification
- **Presentation**: PowerPoint/PDF (final presentation: "Old Greg's Tavern Server Audit, 9.19.25.pdf")
- **Documentation**: HTML and DOCX formats

## Key Insights

### Member Engagement Patterns
- Tracked activation rates by join source
- Identified high-value community members
- Analyzed voice vs. text engagement preferences
- Measured platform-specific participation

### Retention Analysis
- Segmented members by tenure and activity
- Identified churn triggers and at-risk segments
- Measured muter behavior and engagement drop-off
- Created retention cohort analysis

### Growth Opportunities
- Evaluated invite link effectiveness
- Recommended community activation strategies
- Suggested engagement lever optimizations
